TVS Apache RTR 200 4V

Description:

The TVS Apache RTR 200 4V is a dynamic and sporty motorcycle that combines cutting-edge technology with exceptional performance. Designed for enthusiasts seeking an exhilarating riding experience, this bike boasts a perfect blend of power, agility, and innovation.

Details:

Engine: Apache RTR 200 4V is equipped with a high-performance 197.75cc, single-cylinder, 4-stroke, oil-cooled engine, delivering impressive power and torque. It features an advanced fuel injection system for efficient performance across various riding conditions.
Performance: Known for its remarkable performance, the bike delivers a power output of around 20.5 bhp (brake horsepower) at 8500 rpm and torque of 16.8 Nm (Newton-meters) at 7500 rpm. The engine is mated to a smooth 5-speed gearbox.
Design: The Apache RTR 200 4V showcases a sharp and aggressive design language. It features an aerodynamic fuel tank, stylish LED headlamps, a sculpted tail cowl, and a racing-inspired double-barrel exhaust, adding to its sporty appeal.
Technology: Incorporating advanced technology, the bike comes with a fully digital instrument cluster displaying information like speed, tachometer, fuel level, gear indicator, and more. It also features dual-channel ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) for enhanced safety.
Suspension and Handling: The motorcycle is equipped with telescopic front forks and a mono-shock rear suspension, offering a balanced and comfortable ride. The chassis is engineered for optimal stability and handling on various terrains.
Braking System: With a robust braking setup comprising disc brakes on both the front and rear wheels, the Apache RTR 200 4V ensures excellent braking performance and control.
Colors and Variants: Available in various striking color options, the bike offers choices for riders looking to match their style preferences.
